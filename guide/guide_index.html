Snake Bite

What to Do

  • Stay Calm: Call for emergency help immediately.
  • Keep Still: Immobilize the bitten area and keep it lower than the heart.
  • Remove Tight Items: Take off any tight clothing or jewelry near the bite.

What Not to Do

  • No Tourniquet: Don't apply a tourniquet.
  • No Suction: Don't try to suck out the venom.
  • No Ice: Don't apply ice or cold packs.
  • No Alcohol/Caffeine: Avoid giving alcohol or caffeinated drinks.

Seizure/FIDS

What to Do

  • Stay Calm: Keep the person safe and comfortable.
  • Protect Head: Cushion the head to prevent injury.
  • Turn on Side: If possible, turn the person on their side to keep the airway clear.
  • Irom metal: Give the person a Iron metal in hand.

What Not to Do

  • Do Not Restrain: Do not hold the person down.
  • Do Not Put Anything in Mouth: Do not put objects in their mouth.
  • Do Not Panic: Stay calm and speak reassuringly.
  • Do Not Leave Alone: Stay with the person until they recover.
